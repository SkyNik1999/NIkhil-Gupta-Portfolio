MIS771 - Descriptive Analytics And Visualisation

Beautiful Data Inc. Solution

Beautiful Data Inc.

Unit: MIS771_TRI-2_2025

GoodNews Live Dashboard

Use the filters (State, Category, Channel, Membership, Date) to explore sales, profitability, and trends. Hover for tooltips; click marks to highlight related data across charts.

Story-Telling

Design Overview

The dashboard is structured for a Regional Sales Manager: KPI tiles provide an instant health check, followed by regional, categorical, channel and temporal views. A promotions chart tests whether discounts help or harm margins. Filters persist across all views to support quick, targeted questions.

KPI Tiles

Net Sales, Profit, Profit Margin %, Return Rate % summarise performance at a glance. Consistent number formatting (currency/percent) supports quick scanning and comparison after filters are applied.

Regional Performance (Map)

A filled map of Australia encodes Net Sales by state. It quickly surfaces hotspots and gaps; labels and tooltips add Profit and Margin for context. Geographic drill focuses stakeholder attention on where to act.

Category Performance (Bars/Treemap)

Horizontal bars compare Net Sales and show Margin via colour, helping prioritise high-value categories. Sorting reveals leaders immediately; a Format filter (Paperback/Hardcover/eBook) enables a deeper cut when needed.

Channel Comparison (Side-by-Side Bars)

Online vs In-Store performance is displayed side-by-side to guide channel investment decisions. Margin colour highlights profitable mix shifts, while labels show exact values.

Monthly Trend (Line)

A monthly line chart (split by Channel) shows seasonality and the timing of spikes or dips. This supports campaign evaluation and planning for peak periods.

Promotions Impact (Scatter)

Discount % vs Profit Margin % with bubble size = sales tests whether higher discounts translate into profitable sales. Outliers (high discount, low margin) inform more disciplined discounting.

Colour, Hierarchy & UX

A limited palette (sales in blues, margin accents) and consistent encodings reduce cognitive load. Layout follows a top-down narrative: KPIs → region → category/channel → time → promotion effect. Filters are grouped and labelled clearly; tooltips use business language.

Data Preparation (Summary)
  • Verified data types (dates, numeric measures), mapped states to Australia.
  • Created calculations: Profit Margin % = GrossMarginAfterReturns ÷ NetSalesAfterReturns; Return Rate % = ReturnQty ÷ Quantity.
  • Standardised currency/percent formats; resolved any unknown geographies.
Limitations & Next Steps
  • Sample size and historical scope may limit generalisability.
  • Returns and discounts are descriptive, not causal; experiments could confirm uplift.
  • Future work: city/store drill-down, cohort analysis for members, seasonality decomposition.
Accessibility

Labels, tooltips, and adequate contrast improve readability. Keyboard focus is supported by the Tableau embed; alt text provided for the iframe title.
