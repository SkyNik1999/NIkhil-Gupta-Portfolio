Welcome to Beautiful Data Inc.

“Where others see complexity, we see beauty. And value.”

At Beautiful Data Inc., we specialise in transforming raw data into clear, actionable insights through interactive visualisations. Our mission is to help organisations harness the power of data to make informed decisions, improve customer experiences, and drive sustainable growth.

This portfolio showcases our recent project with GoodNews National Bookstore, one of Australia’s largest national retailers. GoodNews operates across major city centres as well as through a dedicated online platform, offering a diverse catalogue of books and serving a wide customer base.

Through this project, our team was tasked with designing an interactive Tableau dashboard that captures book sales transactions, customer behaviours, membership details, product categories, promotions, and financial metrics. The dashboard is designed to provide clarity, support decision-making, and highlight opportunities for strategic improvement.

We invite you to explore the Process and Solution sections of this site, where you will find:

  • A description of the audience and personas guiding the design.
  • Details of the dataset and the elements used.
  • Considerations behind the chosen visualisations.
  • Reflections on ethics and responsible data use.
  • The final Live Dashboard along with storytelling components.

This project reflects Beautiful Data Inc.’s commitment to professionalism, creativity, and innovation in data visualisation.
